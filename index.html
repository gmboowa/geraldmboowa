About Me

I am a scientist passionate about applying genome sequencing technologies to tackle challenges in health and disease, including pathogen characterization, outbreak investigation, and antimicrobial resistance (AMR) surveillance. My research aims to contribute to data science, genomics, and bioinformatics to improve health outcomes globally.

Projects

  • HIV Drug Resistance Analysis

    Using next-generation sequencing to identify genomic markers of resistance and inform treatment strategies.

  • AMR Genomic Surveillance

    Building bioinformatics pipelines for real-time monitoring of antimicrobial resistance determinants.

  • Pathogen Genomics

    Investigating outbreak dynamics through whole-genome sequencing of pathogens.
